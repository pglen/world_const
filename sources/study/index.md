# World Constitution

## Preamble

We, the people of Earth, recognizing our shared humanity, common destiny, and sacred responsibility to present and future generations, hereby establish this World Constitution to promote peace, justice, equality, environmental sustainability, democratic participation, and shared prosperity for all nations and peoples. We affirm that sovereignty is shared responsibility, that Earth's resources are our common heritage, and that all humans possess inherent and inalienable rights.
