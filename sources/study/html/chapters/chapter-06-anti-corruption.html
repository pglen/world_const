Chapter VI: Anti-Corruption and Transparency Framework

Article 20: Citizens' Integrity Chamber

Citizens' Integrity Chamber (CIC) shall be a rotating, lottery-selected citizen panel reviewing high-level decisions. Members shall serve limited terms with comprehensive protection and resources.

Article 21: Radical Transparency

Mandatory publication of all government contracts, budgets, meeting minutes, and lobbying interactions shall be required. Public Ledger Systems using immutable cryptographic logs shall track finances, decision logic, and asset declarations.

Article 22: Whistleblower Protection

Anonymous reporting channels with legal immunity and financial rewards shall be established. Whistleblowers shall receive comprehensive protection and support.

Article 23: Media and Investigative Support

Global Investigative Journalism Trust with firewalled funding and rotating editorial oversight by civil society boards shall be established. Incentives for cross-border investigative collaborations shall be provided.

Article 24: AI Oversight and Ethics

Open-source, auditable algorithms with independent AI Ethics Ombudsman shall govern automated systems. Algorithmic decisions shall be explainable and contestable.
