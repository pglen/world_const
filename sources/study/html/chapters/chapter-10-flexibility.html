Chapter X: Constitutional Flexibility and Development

Article 35: Amendment Process

This Constitution may be amended by:

  • Two-thirds majority of the World Assembly
  • Citizen referendum with ≥60% turnout and ≥66% approval
  • Ratification by three-fourths of member nations through democratic processes

Article 36: Review and Evolution

Every five years, a World Constitutional Convention shall review effectiveness and recommend improvements. Every ten years, a comprehensive review shall consider fundamental restructuring if needed.

Article 37: Emergency Powers

During global emergencies, limited temporary powers may be granted with strict time limits, judicial oversight, and citizen approval through referenda within 90 days.
