Final Provisions. Universal Jurisdiction: Crimes against humanity,
genocide, war crimes, torture, slavery, and ecocide shall be subject
to universal jurisdiction regardless of where committed or by whom.
Scientific Cooperation: Global coordination of scientific research for
pandemics, climate response, energy transitions, and other existential
challenges shall be mandatory. Open-access publishing and equitable
benefit-sharing shall be required. Symbolic Elements: The world shall
adopt symbols representing unity in diversity, including a flag,
anthem, and celebration of World Humanship Day to promote global
identity while respecting local cultures.
