Colophon. This documet mirrors earlier constitutional attempts like the
Constitution for the Federation of Earth (1968–1991) and
structures emerging from proposals by global civil society. It also
builds conceptually from many other documents, like the UN Charter, which
though treaty‑based shares constitutional functions such as universal
scope, fundamental purposes, membership equality ... etc.
This document attempt to embrace modern constitutional models of
open‑source drafting and participatory amendment processes.
This constitution‑style text provides a structured legal
foundation for global governance built on universal rights,
democratic structure, environmental duty, equity, and
participatory evolution.
