Age limit. A human being has a limited lifespan. Towards the end of
life, the faculties degrade. This is a natural process, there is no
acknowledgement or shame is needed. For this reason, an age limit
is imposed on leadership. This age is allocated to the global
mean retirement age of sixty four. (64)
No person should be elected to any governing body at or above this limit,
minus the length of term for that particular office. For instance, a
governing position for a four year term cannot elect any person at
or above the age of 60. This is a necessary arrangement, but the
wisdom of the elders are not lost, as people who are not eligible for
office can still hold advisory positions.
