  Cultural evolution of leader selection. A knowledgeable / qualified person
has a severe disadvantage against a person with a charismatic presence.
Even if the charismatic person has lesser knowledge or lower qualifications.
 In many instances the qualified person is at a disadvantage even when the
charismatic opponent has no relevant qualification(s). In the cultural
evolution we have to morph into a state of mind where qualifications
are ahead of any other trait.

  Another important evolution in leader selection is recognizing harmful
psychological traits of the would be leader. Traits such as excessive
selfishness, narcissism, lack of empathy, arrogance, entitlement
and many other traits. This is important, as people with leadership
aspirations naturally possess these traits. It acts as a driving force
for them. Our task here is to not permit the people who excessively
possess these traits into leadership roles. The seriousness of this
selection anomaly can be seen by examining our current leaders,
and recognizing that almost all of them qualify for excessive harmful
psychological traits.
